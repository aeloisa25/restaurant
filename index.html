Food is a big part of the Eloisa family and our goal is to fill every stomach with happiness and joy. Here at Taco Land, we value fresh ingredients, traditional techniques and great customer service, Almost as if you were back at grandmas. We are located in the new downtown section of Wheeling,IL right off Dundee Rd. Come in and try a piece of our history and our culture and feel right at home.
